ABOUT UNIVAULT
What is UniVault?

Univault is an academic project for COMP 010: Information Management, focused on designing a relational database system based on BDO Unibank Inc.’s A1–A2: Customer Information, Regulations, and Agreements form. Used for opening or updating accounts, the form collects key customer details such as name, contact info, and employment.

By analyzing the form’s structure, the group identified its core entities and relationships to create essential documentation, including an ERD, normalization tables, and a data dictionary. The result is a scalable and well-organized database that demonstrates practical knowledge of relational database design in a real-world banking context.
